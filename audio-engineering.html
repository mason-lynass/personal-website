To celebrate my graduation from the University of Washington, I presented some music I had been studying and practicing for more than a year!

Here's my recording of The Fall of the Empire, Frederic Rzewski's eight movement speaking percussionist solo, originally composed for Allen Otte in 2007.

I made this video of Tapping the Furnace, a speaking percussionist solo composed by Dorothy Hindman in 2007.

This is the first movement of Steve Reich's "New York Counterpoint."

Here's my performance of the quartet version of my composition "Tesserae." This piece won the Portland Percussion Group Call for Scores in 2017, and is composed with flexibility in mind: it can be performed as a quartet, or a trio (without the drumset part).
